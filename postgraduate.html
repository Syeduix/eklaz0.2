Postgraduate FAQs

Ask Us Directly →
Do I need a first-class undergraduate degree?
Most master's require a 2:1 or international equivalent. Some accept 2:2 with strong work experience or a related dissertation. A few top programmes prefer a first.
How much does a UK master's cost?
International fees range £18,000–£40,000 for the year. MBAs at top schools can reach £60,000+. Living costs add £12,000–£18,000 depending on city.
Do I need work experience for a master's?
Most MSc and MA programmes accept fresh graduates. MBAs typically require 3+ years of professional experience. We'll be honest about what fits your profile.
What about the GMAT or GRE?
Required for many US programmes and most MBAs globally. Most UK MSc programmes don't require either. We'll confirm course-by-course.
Can I work after graduation in the UK?
Yes — the UK Graduate Route gives master's graduates 2 years of post-study work rights, regardless of whether you have a job offer.
