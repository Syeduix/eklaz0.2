Before You Start

Documents you'll need to gather

Have these ready and the timeline gets dramatically smoother. Don't have something? We'll guide you through how to get it.

  • Academic transcripts From all schools/colleges attended in the last 5 years.
  • English proficiency test IELTS, TOEFL, PTE, or Duolingo (or we can arrange one).
  • Personal statement / SoP We help draft and refine this from scratch.
  • Two academic references From recent teachers, tutors, or employers.
  • Valid passport With at least 18 months remaining on it.
  • Financial documents Bank statements or sponsor letters (exact format guidance provided).
Common Questions

We're happy to answer

If your question isn't here, just send a message — we usually reply within one working day.

Ask Us Directly →
How much does it cost to work with E-Klaz?
The initial consultation is free. Our service fee depends on the level of support you need — full end-to-end or specific stages. You'll get a clear, fixed quote before any work begins.
Do you guarantee admission to a specific university?
No reputable consultancy can — admission decisions belong solely to universities. What we guarantee is the strongest possible application, honest advice on your chances, and a 95% overall success rate across our students.
When should I start the application process?
For UK September intakes, ideally 9–12 months ahead. For January intakes, 6 months ahead. Earlier is always better for scholarship access.
Can you help with visa applications?
Yes — visa support is included in our full-service package. We provide checklists, CAS guidance, financial evidence formatting, and interview prep.
What if my grades aren't quite high enough?
We have foundation and pathway programmes designed for exactly this situation — they're a legitimate route into top universities.
Do you help with scholarships?
Always. We maintain an active database of country-specific, subject-specific, and merit-based scholarships, and help you apply to every one you qualify for.
