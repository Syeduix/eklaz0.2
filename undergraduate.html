What You'll Need

Entry requirements at a glance

Requirements vary by course and university, but here's what most undergraduate applications expect from international students.

  • Academic qualifications A-levels (typically AAB–A*AA for top unis), IB (32–38 points), or equivalent. Foundation route available if you don't have these.
  • English proficiency IELTS 6.0–7.0 overall (depending on course), TOEFL 80–100, or equivalent.
  • Personal statement A 4,000-character essay showing your motivation and fit. We help you draft and edit.
  • One academic reference From a recent teacher or tutor. We brief them on what UK admissions tutors look for.
  • Portfolio (creative courses) Art, design, architecture, and music applications require a portfolio or audition.
