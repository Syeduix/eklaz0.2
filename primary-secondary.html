Ask Us Directly →
What does UK boarding school cost?
Typically £35,000–£55,000 per year for top boarding schools, all-inclusive (tuition, accommodation, meals, most activities). We help you understand the full cost and surface any available bursaries or scholarships.
How young can my child board?
Most boarding schools take students from age 8, though some accept from age 7. We help you assess whether boarding is right at each stage.
Do you arrange guardianship for under-18s?
Yes — UKCISA-registered guardianship is a legal requirement for international students under 18 and we handle it as part of our service.
When should we apply?
For September entry, ideally 18 months ahead for top schools. Selective schools have limited places and early entrance exams.
What about scholarships?
Academic, music, sport, art, and all-rounder scholarships are available at most independent schools. Awards typically cover 10–50% of fees.
