WHAT WE BELIEVE

That no qualified student should be held back by paperwork, distance, or cost. That advice should be honest, even when it's hard to hear. And that placement is the start, not the end, of our work together.

Our Journey

From a weekend side-project to a global consultancy

2014

The first cohort

Our founders — both UK-educated themselves — started weekend advice sessions for students in Lagos. 12 placements in year one.

2017

London office opens

Headquartered at 138C Benares Road. First full-time advisors join. Coverage expands across the UK Russell Group.

2019

Beyond the UK

Partnerships with universities in Canada, Australia, and the US. First Chevening scholarship secured for an E-Klaz student.

2022

3,000th student placed

Expanded into postgraduate research with dedicated PhD advisory and supervisor-matching service.

2026

5,000+ and counting

Today, students from 30+ countries trust E-Klaz with their education journey. 95% application success rate. The mission hasn't changed.

How We Work

Four principles that shape every conversation

— 01 / TRUTH

We tell students what they need to hear

If your dream programme isn't realistic this year, we'll tell you — and we'll show you a route that gets you there next year. Honesty beats commission, every time.

— 02 / FIT

The right university, not the famous one

A perfectly-matched mid-ranked programme beats a misfit at a top-10. We optimise for your trajectory, not our trophy cabinet.

— 03 / SUPPORT

Placement isn't the finish line

We stay with you through visa, pre-departure, and your first term. Most of our students stay in touch for years afterwards.

— 04 / CLARITY

No surprise fees, ever

Free initial consultation. A fixed, written quote before any work begins. You'll always know exactly what you're paying for.
